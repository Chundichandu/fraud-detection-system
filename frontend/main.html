How Our AI Analysis Works

Our advanced fraud detection system uses machine learning to analyze multiple risk factors in real-time:

  • Transaction Amount: Analyzes if the amount is unusually high or follows suspicious patterns
  • Geographic Risk: Evaluates the country of origin and associated fraud risk levels
  • Timing Analysis: Considers time of day and unusual transaction timing patterns
  • Account Validation: Verifies IFSC codes and account number formats for authenticity
  • Pattern Recognition: Detects sequential or repetitive patterns that may indicate fraud

Each transaction receives a risk score from 0-100%. Transactions above 75% are automatically declined, 40-75% are flagged for review, and below 40% are approved.
